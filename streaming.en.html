📢 Summary
Streaming, Let's Plays, clips, and monetization of this game are all permitted without prior permission.
Whether you are an individual or a company, feel free to share your content within these guidelines.

✅ What You Are Free to Do

Streaming & Let's Plays OK Stream and upload freely on any platform — YouTube, Twitch, Niconico, X (Twitter), TikTok, and others.
Monetization (ads, super chats, etc.) OK Any revenue earned from videos or streams is yours to keep. No prior permission and no payment to us are required.
Clip/Highlight Videos OK You may create and publish clip/highlight videos, whether of your own content or someone else's.
* If you clip another creator's stream or video, we cannot take responsibility for any disputes between you and the original creator or rights holder. Please also check the original creator's terms and permissions.
Screenshot Use OK Feel free to use in-game screenshots for thumbnails, social media posts, blog articles, and similar purposes.
Paid/Subscriber-Only Streams OK Use in members-only videos or subscription-based streams is fine.
Competitions & Events OK You may use this game in contests and events, both online and offline.

🙏 Requests (Optional)

Credit

Not required, but we would greatly appreciate it if you include the game's name and Steam store URL in your video description or stream title — it helps viewers find the game.

Example:
Game: Railway Dispatch Simulator
Steam: https://store.steampowered.com/app/4282480/

Social Media Hashtags

When posting on X (Twitter), TikTok, and similar platforms, we'd love it if you used the following hashtags:

  • #RailwayDispatchSimulator
  • #TrainSim

The developer (@tadi_maru) may repost or drop a greeting when they spot your post.

⚠️ What Is Not Allowed

Distribution/Re-upload of Game Files NG Distributing or re-uploading the game's installer, executable, or data files on other sites is prohibited.
Modification & Reverse Engineering NG Disassembling the game or distributing modified data is prohibited. MOD distribution is also not supported at this time.
Use Against Public Decency NG Use for discriminatory, defamatory, or illegal purposes is prohibited.
Impersonating the Developer NG Falsely claiming to be "official" or "developer-endorsed" (for example, implying sponsorship by a specific company or municipality) is prohibited.

📋 For Companies & Large-Scale Streamers

These guidelines apply equally to individuals and companies. No prior application or separate contract is required.

However, if you are considering any of the following large-scale promotional use cases, please reach out in advance so we can avoid surprises:

  • Use in broadcast television programs
  • Large-scale features at streaming events with audiences of 500,000+
  • Publications or merchandise based on this game
  • Use involving sponsorships or tie-ins
